Recent data suggests that EdTech startups in Southeast Asia (Singapore, Indonesia, Vietnam)
have secured over $400M in seed funding in the first half of 2024 alone. Investors are 
pivoting towards AI-native learning platforms that solve language barrier issues 
across the region. East Ventures remains the most active seed-stage investor in this niche.
