### 2024 Market Overview
India's healthcare venture capital landscape saw a significant upturn in 2024, with total funding reaching $1.13 billion. Early-stage startups dominated the volume, securing $250.44 million across 72 deals.

### Key Active Investors
1. **Inflection Point Ventures (IPV)**: Led a $700K seed round for Neuranics.
2. **Endiya Partners**: Actively funding early-stage healthcare and lifesciences.
3. **Eximius Ventures**: Focusing on pre-seed and seed HealthTech.
4. **z21 Ventures**: Recently closed a $20M tranche targeting seed-stage healthcare.
5. **Kae Capital**: Participating in rounds for high-growth HealthTech like Qure.ai.

### Emerging Trends
- **AI in Healthcare**: Significant momentum for diagnostics and personalized treatment platforms.
- **Chronic Pain Management**: FlexifyMe raised INR 10 crore in seed funding for AI-based pain management.
- **Diagnostics**: Neuranics diagnostics platform secured $700K to expand Tier 2/3 city penetration.
