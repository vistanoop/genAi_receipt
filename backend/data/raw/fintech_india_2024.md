### 2024 Market Dynamics
The Indian fintech sector remained the third-highest funded globally in Q1 2024. While overall funding is robust, seed-stage deals saw a 75% decline as investors shifted preference toward late-stage "proven" models.

### Top Early-Stage Investors
- **Blume Ventures**: Announced 58 deals in 2024, with a heavy skew toward FinTech and SaaS.
- **Peak XV Partners**: Remains the most active lead investor in the region.
- **DeVC**: Completed 60 deals in 2024 with ticket sizes ranging from $50K to $500K.
- **Nexus Venture Partners**: Recently participated in a $25.5 million seed round for Zinc Money alongside Quona Capital.

### High-Growth Sub-sectors
1. **Alternative Lending**: Digital lending solutions accounted for 64% of total funding.
2. **RegTech**: Increased focus on compliance automation for the Indian market.
3. **Banking Tech**: Strong performance in Q1 2024 infrastructure plays.
