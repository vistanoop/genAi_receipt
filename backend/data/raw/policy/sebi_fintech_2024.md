SEBI has announced a new framework for digital payment aggregators in India. Starting Q1 2024,
startups in the Fintech space will benefit from streamlined licensing procedures for
cross-border transactions. This move is expected to attract significant Series A and B
capital from global investors looking at the Indian digital economy.
