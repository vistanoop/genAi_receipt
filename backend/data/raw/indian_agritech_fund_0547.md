Arya.ag, an integrated agri-services platform, has raised $60 million in a Series C funding round. The investment was led by Lightrock India, with participation from existing investors Quasta Capital, responsAbility, Asia Impact SA, and Omidyar Network India. The funds will be used to expand Arya.ag's warehousing capacity, deepen its presence in the agri-finance space, and strengthen its technology infrastructure.  The company focuses on providing post-harvest services like warehousing, finance, and market linkages to smallholder farmers, FPOs, and agri-enterprises.
